How to use accordions

This component is one of 2 components youâ€™ll require to form an expanding accordion:

  • Accordion Heading
  • End of Accordion Group (this component)

Insert the Accordion Heading component first, give it a name, and then go back to your app screen.

On your app screen, hit enter after the heading and add all the content for your accordion directly underneath the heading.

Add more headings and content if required.

Once you have all Accordion Heading components and content, add a bottom end to your accordion with this component.

You only need one End of Accordion Group component for every group of consecutive accordions.

For more information and inspiration, read Fliplet's guide to accordions.
